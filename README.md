# Andrea Adofo Asiedu — Portfolio Website

Welcome to my portfolio website repository!

## What is this website?

This site is a showcase of my work as a digital business student and aspiring entrepreneur who bridges the worlds of technology, business strategy, and design. The portfolio highlights key projects, technical and business skills, and provides clear, actionable ways for visitors to connect—whether you're a potential employer, collaborator, or client. Built with a strong focus on usability and accessibility, it aims to tell my story through impactful visuals and straightforward navigation.

## Goals

- Present a credible, professional digital identity
- Let employers quickly assess my skills/projects
- Make it easy for clients & collaborators to engage
- Ensure great accessibility and smooth experience on every device
